# Code of Conduct

Rx Shortage Radar is a small public-data project. Keep discussion practical, respectful, and focused on improving the tool.

## Be Constructive

- Be direct, but do not be rude.
- Keep feedback specific enough that someone can act on it.
- Help new contributors understand the codebase and public data sources.
- Do not post private data.

## Not Allowed

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Secrets, PHI, credentials, private files, screenshots of private systems, or private operational paths.
- Requests for private clinical, pharmacy, vendor, or hospital data.
- Repeated off-topic or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or block accounts when needed.

For possible security issues or sensitive-data exposure, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) instead of posting details publicly.
